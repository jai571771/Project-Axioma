2009

The Beginning

Our journey started with a simple belief – that great design can transform lives. We established our first architectural studio in Mumbai with only 3 passionate visionaries, dedicated to challenging standard housing structures and bringing premium aesthetics to high-density living.

2012

Growing Vision

Expanded our team and capabilities, taking on diverse projects across multiple sectors. During this phase, we completed our first major commercial complex which was awarded for sustainable building designs, expanding our footprint to major metro cities in the country.

2015

New Horizons

Ventured into international projects, bringing our design philosophy to global clients. Partnering with elite European designers, we developed flagship beachfront resorts and premium residential villas that blended local nature with luxury structural aesthetics.

2019

Leading the Way

Recognized for design excellence and innovation in architecture and construction. We won our first major international award and integrated advanced building technologies like smart climate facades and prefabricated eco-concrete structures.

2024+

Shaping the Future

Continuing to push boundaries and create sustainable, timeless architectural solutions. With our expanded global team of 120+ designers, we are actively developing carbon-neutral skyscrapers and immersive luxury estates that redefine the human living experience.

What Drives Us

Experience That Makes a Difference

Deep Expertise

Years of hands-on experience across residential, commercial and hospitality projects. We know the technical requirements for complex luxury structural engineering inside and out.

Innovative Thinking

We blend creativity with functionality to deliver designs that inspire. By rethinking standard spatial logic, we create architectural narratives that surprise and engage.

Uncompromising Quality

From concept to completion, quality is at the heart of everything we do. We source premium materials globally and collaborate only with highly-skilled structural craftsmen.

Sustainable Approach

Designing spaces that respect the environment and future generations. We prioritize natural airflow, energy efficiency, carbon-neutral materials, and local biophilic styling.
